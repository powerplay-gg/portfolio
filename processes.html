New processes of design team

Loginet, 2021

As a manager, I had to improve old and implement new processes in the design department

Problem

In May 2021, my teamlead quit and I started working alone. For two months we could not find either a new leader or a middle designer. Realizing that it was impossible to continue working like this, I initiated the hiring of a junior designers. Having hired two juniors, I realized that a number of new processes needed to be introduced.

Solution

Sprints

Previously, we worked without tasks and sprints, the situation allowed. After hiring two new designers and increasing the number of projects, I introduced sprint work with task tracking. At the beginning of each week, we called the PO and synchronized the tasks: what was done and what needs to be done, determined the pool of tasks for the week.

Weekly retrospectives

For better adaptation and growth of employees, I started holding weekly 1:1 meetings, where we discuss the past week with the designer, what tasks were completed, where there were problems, we determine growth points and make a plan for the next week.

Design debt

Over the 2 years of the existence of a new design of the product, many problems have accumulated in the technical implementation of the intended design. Given the intensity of the growth in the number and complexity of projects, in the near future we could face either a deterioration in the quality of design, or an increase in development time. I compiled a list of problems, such as inaccuracies in layouts, outdated approaches to interface design, usability issues, etc. All these problems are framed in tasks and distributed in sprints.

Creation of a complete design system

Our product grew and without clearly defined rules, the design could not exist. We defined them with my previous teamlead, but there was no unified knowledge base and description of the operation of the elements and patterns of user interaction. The guidelines helped new employees to quickly adapt to the company, simplified the interaction between departments and reduced the number of calls to designers. In November 2021, we presented the structure of our guidelines and a description of several elements of the team.
